Welcome to the **Edgaze Blog**. This is where we share what we're building, how creators are using Edgaze, and where the AI creator economy is headed.

## Why we're starting a blog

We've seen creators build incredible workflows, sell prompts, and grow audiences on Edgaze. A blog gives us a space to tell those stories, explain new features, and share our vision for the platform.

## What to expect

- **Product updates** — New features, improvements, and what’s coming next
- **Creator spotlights** — How builders are using Edgaze to create and sell AI products
- **Tips and guides** — Best practices for workflows, prompts, and distribution
- **Industry insights** — Thoughts on the AI creator economy and where it’s going

## Stay connected

Follow along for updates. If you’re building with Edgaze, we’d love to hear from you.

Thanks for being part of this journey.
