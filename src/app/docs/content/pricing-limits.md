# Pricing Limits

_Last Updated: March 13, 2026_

Creators set the price of their products within defined limits. These limits help maintain marketplace quality, prevent spam listings, and avoid extreme pricing.

## Prompt Pricing

### Allowed Range for Prompts

| Limit             | Amount |
| ----------------- | ------ |
| **Minimum price** | $3     |
| **Maximum price** | $50    |

## Workflow Pricing

### Allowed Range for Workflows

| Limit             | Amount                                              |
| ----------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| **Minimum price** | $5 (plus estimated infrastructure cost for 10 runs) |
| **Maximum price** | $150                                                |

### How Workflow Minimums Are Calculated

For workflows, the minimum price includes a base of $5 plus the estimated infrastructure cost shown at publish time. This infrastructure cost is **separate from** the Edgaze marketplace fee (20%). See [Infrastructure Cost Estimation](/docs/infrastructure-cost-estimation) for details.

## Why Limits Exist

### Marketplace Goals

- **Prevent spam** — Very low prices can lead to low-quality or spam listings
- **Avoid extremes** — Reasonable bounds keep the marketplace coherent
- **Quality signal** — Pricing within a sensible range supports buyer confidence

## Currency

### Pricing Currency

All prices are set and displayed in **USD**. Stripe may support conversion for international customers; conversion and fees are handled by the payment processor.

## Updates to Limits

### Future Changes

Edgaze may update pricing limits in the future to reflect marketplace conditions, product categories, or platform evolution. Material changes will be communicated with notice. Existing products may be grandfathered or required to comply with new limits—specifics will be communicated at the time of any change.
