1. Purpose of These Guidelines

These Creator Guidelines (“Guidelines”) define expectations and requirements for users who create, upload, publish, or manage content on the Edgaze platform (the “Platform”).

The Platform is owned and operated by **Edge Platforms, Inc.**, a Delaware C Corporation.


These Guidelines are intended to:
- Maintain platform integrity and safety
- Reduce legal, regulatory, and reputational risk
- Clarify acceptable creator conduct
- Supplement the Terms of Service and Acceptable Use Policy

Violation of these Guidelines may result in enforcement action, including warnings, content removal, suspension, or permanent account termination.

---

2. Applicability

These Guidelines apply to all creators, publishers, and contributors on the Platform, regardless of account type or access level.

By publishing content on the Platform, you agree to comply with these Guidelines.

---

3. Creator Responsibility

Creators are solely responsible for:
- The content they publish
- Ensuring compliance with applicable laws and regulations
- Ensuring content does not violate third-party rights
- Understanding risks associated with AI, automation, and external tools

Edge Platforms does not assume responsibility for creator content.

---

4. Prohibited Content

Creators must not publish content that includes or relates to any of the following.

4.1 Illegal or Unlawful Content

- Content that violates any applicable law or regulation
- Instructions facilitating criminal activity
- Circumvention of legal or regulatory safeguards

---

4.2 Fraud, Scams, and Deceptive Practices

- False or misleading claims
- Impersonation of individuals or organizations
- Phishing, social engineering, or deceptive automation
- Claims of guaranteed results, profits, or outcomes

---

4.3 Harmful, Abusive, or Inappropriate Content

- Harassment, threats, or intimidation
- Hate speech or discriminatory conduct
- Promotion of violence, terrorism, or extremist ideology
- Sexually explicit or pornographic material
- Content intended to shock, exploit, or harm users

---

4.4 Intellectual Property Violations

- Unauthorized use of copyrighted material
- Distribution of proprietary or confidential information
- Circumvention of licenses or access controls

---

4.5 Privacy and Data Violations

- Unauthorized collection or disclosure of personal data
- Doxxing or exposure of sensitive personal information
- Content violating privacy or data protection obligations

---

4.6 Malware, Exploits, and System Abuse

- Malicious code or harmful payloads
- Exploits targeting software, platforms, or users
- Attempts to bypass platform safeguards or restrictions

---

4.7 Misuse of AI and Automation

- Use of AI or automation for unlawful surveillance
- Deceptive or manipulative automated behavior
- Automation designed to violate platform rules or third-party terms

---

5. Quality and Accuracy Expectations

While Edge Platforms does not guarantee quality or accuracy, creators are expected to:
- Avoid intentionally misleading or deceptive content
- Clearly describe limitations and assumptions where applicable
- Avoid presenting experimental or speculative content as fact

Edge Platforms does not verify or endorse creator content.

---

6. Enforcement Framework

Edge Platforms reserves the right, but not the obligation, to enforce these Guidelines.

Enforcement actions may include, at Edgaze’s sole discretion:
- Content removal or restriction
- Temporary account suspension
- Permanent account termination

---

7. Warning and Ban Policy

For inappropriate or prohibited content:

- First violation  
  - Content may be removed  
  - A warning notice may be sent to the creator via email  

- Second violation  
  - Permanent account suspension or ban  
  - Loss of publishing and access privileges  

Edgaze is not required to issue warnings prior to enforcement where immediate action is warranted.

---

8. No Obligation to Monitor

Edge Platforms:
- Does not actively monitor all creator content
- Does not guarantee detection of violations
- Is not obligated to review or investigate every report

Enforcement may be discretionary, delayed, or limited.

---

9. Appeals and Reinstatement

Edge Platforms may, but is not required to, review appeals or reinstatement requests.

No guarantee is made regarding review timelines or outcomes.

---

10. No Creator Rights or Guarantees

Publication on the Platform does not create:
- Any entitlement to distribution, visibility, or promotion
- Any partnership, employment, or agency relationship
- Any guarantee of continued access or publishing privileges

---

11. Relationship to Other Policies

These Guidelines operate in conjunction with:
- Terms of Service
- Privacy Policy
- Acceptable Use Policy
- Content Disclaimer
- DMCA and Intellectual Property Takedown Policy
- Platform Status and Beta Disclaimer

In the event of conflict, the Terms of Service control.

---

12. Changes to These Guidelines

These Guidelines may be updated at any time.

Changes become effective upon publication. Continued use of the Platform constitutes acceptance of the updated Guidelines.
