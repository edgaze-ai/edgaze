1. Purpose and Legal Effect

These Community Guidelines constitute a binding component of the Terms of Service for the Edgaze platform (the “Platform”).

The Platform is owned and operated by **Edge Platforms, Inc.**, a Delaware C Corporation.

They set forth minimum standards of acceptable conduct, content, and interaction across the Platform.

Failure to comply may result in removal of content, suspension, or termination of access, at Edge Platforms’ sole discretion.

Participation on Edgaze is conditioned upon compliance with these Guidelines.

---

2. Scope of Application

These Guidelines apply to all persons who access or use Edgaze, including but not limited to:
- Registered users and guests  
- Creators, workflow publishers, and contributors  
- Commenters, reviewers, and moderators  
- Developers, integrators, and automated agents  

They govern all activity on the Platform, including:
- Workflows, prompts, templates, and assets  
- Comments, replies, reviews, and ratings  
- User profiles, usernames, avatars, and bios  
- Public and private communications  
- API usage, automation, and third-party integrations  

By using Edgaze, you affirm that you have read, understood, and agreed to these Guidelines.

---

3. Prohibited Content (Zero Tolerance)

The following content is strictly prohibited and may result in immediate suspension or permanent ban, without prior notice:

You must not create, upload, distribute, promote, or facilitate:
- Hate speech, harassment, or discriminatory content targeting protected groups  
- Threats, violence, or incitement to harm  
- Sexual content, pornography, or explicit material  
- Child sexual abuse material or grooming behavior  
- Extremist or terrorist propaganda  
- Self-harm or suicide encouragement  
- False or misleading information likely to cause real-world harm  
- Deepfakes or synthetic media intended to deceive, defame, or manipulate  
- Illegal activities or tools that meaningfully facilitate wrongdoing  
- Content that violates applicable laws or regulations  

Edge Platforms reserves the right to determine, in its sole discretion, whether content violates this section.

---

4. Truthfulness and Integrity of Content

All content published on Edgaze must be accurate, honest, and presented in good faith.

You must not:
- Claim ownership of content you did not create  
- Repackage, resell, or redistribute others’ work without authorization  
- Misrepresent what a workflow does or its performance  
- Use deceptive titles, thumbnails, or descriptions  
- Fabricate or manipulate metrics, ratings, or testimonials  
- Engage in clickbait, bait-and-switch, or misleading monetization practices  

Edge Platforms may remove misleading or deceptive content without notice.

---

5. Prohibited Platform Abuse

You must not:
- Attempt to bypass paywalls, safeguards, or restrictions  
- Exploit bugs, vulnerabilities, or loopholes  
- Use bots, scripts, or automation to manipulate engagement  
- Create multiple accounts to evade bans or limits  
- Scrape, harvest, or misuse user data without authorization  
- Interfere with, disrupt, or degrade Platform operations  

Edge Platforms reserves the right to take technical and legal action against abuse.

---

6. Commenting and Review Standards (Explicit Section)

All comments, replies, and reviews must comply with these standards.

You must not post comments that are:
- Harassing, abusive, or defamatory  
- Personal attacks against individuals or groups  
- Hateful, discriminatory, or demeaning  
- Sexually explicit or inappropriate  
- Threatening, violent, or inciting harm  
- False, misleading, or maliciously damaging  
- Spam, repetitive, or irrelevant  
- Commercial solicitation or advertising without permission  

Constructive criticism is permitted. Harassment is not.

Edge Platforms may:
- Remove comments at its discretion  
- Disable commenting on specific workflows or profiles  
- Suspend or ban users for repeated violations  

Posting comments is a privilege, not a right.

---

7. Privacy, Data Protection, and Doxxing

You must not:
- Share private or personal information about others without consent  
- Doxx, stalk, or target individuals  
- Upload confidential, proprietary, or sensitive data  
- Collect or misuse user data obtained through the Platform  

Any violation of this section may result in immediate termination.

---

8. Creator Responsibilities and Liability

Creators are solely responsible for their published content.

You must ensure:
- You own or have rights to all materials you publish  
- Your content complies with applicable laws  
- Your workflows do not infringe third-party rights  
- Your outputs do not facilitate illegal or harmful activity  

Edge Platforms does not assume liability for creator misconduct.

---

9. Moderation and Enforcement

Edge Platforms reserves the right, at its sole discretion, to:
- Remove any content for any reason  
- Suspend or terminate accounts  
- Restrict access to features or monetization  
- Demonetize or disable workflows  
- Ban users permanently  

Enforcement decisions are final and not subject to appeal unless explicitly stated.
