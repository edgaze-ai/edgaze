## Initial Platform Release  
**1 January 2026**

This marks the first public availability of the Edgaze platform in **closed beta**.  
All features listed below are experimental and subject to change.

---

### Core Platform

- Launched the foundational Edgaze platform as a **neutral content and discovery layer** for AI prompts, workflows, and tools.
- Introduced a creator-first publishing flow designed for rapid iteration and testing.
- Enabled public discovery of content through the Edgaze marketplace during the closed beta phase.
- Established Edgaze as a **platform and intermediary**, not a content creator or service provider.

---

### Marketplace (Closed Beta)

- Released the initial version of the Edgaze Marketplace for testing and feedback.
- All marketplace content is **free to access** during closed beta.
- Payments, monetization, and financial transactions are **disabled** at this stage.
- Marketplace listings are user-generated and not verified by Edgaze.
- Added basic metadata support for listings (title, description, creator attribution).

---

### Docs & Policies

- Introduced the Edgaze Docs system as a centralized source for:
  - Terms of Service
  - Privacy Policy
  - Platform changelogs
- Docs are designed to evolve alongside the platform and reflect current practices.
- Established a clear separation between product UI and documentation UI.

---

### Creator Experience

- Enabled creators to publish AI-related content for testing and sharing.
- Content can be edited, unpublished, or removed by the creator.
- No guarantees are made regarding visibility, reach, or performance of published content.
- Creator tools are experimental and may change without notice.

---

### Platform Architecture

- Deployed the first production-grade infrastructure layer for Edgaze.
- Implemented core authentication, routing, and content delivery mechanisms.
- Introduced internal logging and monitoring for stability and abuse prevention.
- No guarantees are made regarding uptime, data retention, or backward compatibility during closed beta.

---

### AI and Automation Disclaimer

- The platform may surface or host AI-generated or AI-assisted content.
- Edgaze does not validate, endorse, or guarantee the accuracy or safety of AI outputs.
- Users are responsible for evaluating suitability before use or deployment.

---

### Known Limitations

- The platform is under active development and may contain bugs or incomplete features.
- Data loss, UI inconsistencies, or unexpected behavior may occur.
- Documentation may lag behind actual platform behavior during rapid iteration.
- Support is limited during closed beta.

---

### What’s Next

- Refinement of creator workflows based on early feedback.
- Gradual rollout of advanced discovery and organization features.
- Evaluation of future monetization and payment infrastructure.
- Continued hardening of platform stability and security.

---

This changelog represents the **starting point** of Edgaze.  
Future updates will be incremental, transparent, and documented here.
