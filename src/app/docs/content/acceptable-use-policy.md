# Acceptable Use Policy

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") defines prohibited uses of the Edgaze platform (the "Platform") and supplements the Terms of Service.

The Platform is owned and operated by **Edge Platforms, Inc.**, a Delaware Corporation.

## Purpose of This Policy

This Policy exists to:

- Protect the Platform, its users, and third parties
- Limit legal, regulatory, and operational risk
- Preserve Edge Platforms' status as a neutral intermediary

Violation of this AUP may result in content removal, account suspension, or termination, at Edge Platforms' sole discretion.

## Applicability

This AUP applies to:

- All users of the Platform
- All content submitted, published, or shared
- All interactions, tools, workflows, and usage patterns

Use of the Platform constitutes acceptance of this Policy.

## General Use Requirements

You agree to use the Platform only for lawful purposes and in compliance with:

- Applicable laws and regulations
- The Terms of Service
- This Acceptable Use Policy

You are solely responsible for your conduct and content.

## Prohibited Content and Activities

You may not use the Platform to create, upload, publish, share, or distribute content or engage in activity that includes or relates to any of the following.

### Illegal or Unlawful Activity

- Any activity that violates local, national, or international law
- Content facilitating or encouraging criminal acts
- Circumvention of legal safeguards or regulatory requirements

### Fraud, Deception, and Scams

- Fraudulent schemes or misrepresentation
- Impersonation of individuals or organizations
- Phishing, social engineering, or deceptive practices
- False claims regarding outcomes, earnings, or guarantees

### Malware, Exploits, and Harmful Code

- Viruses, worms, trojans, or malicious payloads
- Exploits targeting software, systems, or networks
- Instructions designed to bypass safeguards or security controls

### Intellectual Property Infringement

- Content that infringes copyrights, trademarks, patents, or trade secrets
- Unauthorized use or redistribution of proprietary materials
- Circumvention of licensing or access controls

### Privacy and Data Protection Violations

- Unauthorized collection, use, or disclosure of personal data
- Content that violates privacy, confidentiality, or data protection laws
- Doxxing or disclosure of sensitive personal information

### Harmful or Abusive Conduct

- Harassment, threats, or intimidation
- Hate speech or discriminatory conduct
- Promotion of violence, terrorism, or extremist ideology

### Misuse of AI and Automation

- Use of AI workflows or tools for unlawful surveillance
- Generation of deceptive, harmful, or misleading content
- Use of automation to violate laws, platform rules, or third-party terms

Edge Platforms does not validate or approve AI outputs. Responsibility rests entirely with the user.

### Platform Abuse and Circumvention

- Attempting to bypass platform safeguards or restrictions
- Excessive automated access or scraping
- Reverse engineering, probing, or interfering with the Platform
