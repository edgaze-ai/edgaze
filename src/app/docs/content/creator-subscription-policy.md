## 1. Edgaze Plus

*Last Updated: March 13, 2026*

Creators may optionally subscribe to **Edgaze Plus**, a paid tier that provides additional benefits for workflow creators.

---

## 2. Price

**$20 per month**

Billed monthly. Creators can cancel at any time.

---

## 3. Benefits

Edgaze Plus includes:

| Benefit | Free | Plus |
|---------|------|------|
| Hosted runs per purchase | 10 | 15 |
| Advanced builder features | — | ✓ |
| Private workflows | — | ✓ |
| Workflow versioning | — | ✓ |
| Detailed analytics | — | ✓ |
| Priority support | — | ✓ |

### 3.1 Hosted Runs

Plus creators offer **15 hosted runs** per workflow purchase instead of 10. This can improve conversion and customer satisfaction.

### 3.2 Advanced Builder Features

Access to additional workflow builder capabilities, templates, and tools.

### 3.3 Private Workflows

Ability to publish workflows as private (invite-only or unlisted) in addition to public marketplace listings.

### 3.4 Workflow Versioning

Version control for workflows, allowing creators to manage updates and rollbacks.

### 3.5 Detailed Analytics

Enhanced analytics on workflow performance, usage, and revenue.

### 3.6 Priority Support

Faster response times and dedicated support channels for Plus creators.

---

## 4. Subscription Management

- Billing is handled through Stripe
- Subscriptions renew automatically each month
- Creators can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time
- Refunds for subscription fees are handled according to our [Refund Policy](/docs/refund-policy)

---

## 5. Effect on Existing Workflows

When a creator subscribes to Plus:

- New workflow purchases include 15 runs (instead of 10)
- Existing customer purchases retain their original run allocation
- When a creator cancels Plus, they revert to the free tier; existing customers keep their purchased runs

---

## 6. Tax

Subscription fees may be subject to applicable sales tax or VAT depending on your location. Tax is calculated at checkout.
