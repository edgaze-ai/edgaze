## [1.0.0] — 2025-02-10

### Added
- **Prompt Studio** — Structured prompts with placeholders; versioning and one-click publish with a dedicated product page.
- **Workflow Builder** — Visual flows (inputs, prompts, tools, outputs); single canvas, single link, repeatable runs.
- **Marketplace** — Curated discovery, search, and product pages; storefront for creators, one-tap open and run for users.
- **Edgaze codes & links** — Short codes (e.g. `@handle/essay`) and canonical URLs for every prompt and workflow.
- **Authentication** — Supabase Auth: email/password and Google OAuth.
- **Pricing** — Creators can set prices; payment activation planned for a future release.
